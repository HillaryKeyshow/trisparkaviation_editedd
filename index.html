Helicopter Charter

Our helicopter charter service offers fast, comfortable, and flexible travel to some of Kenya’s most stunning destinations. Whether you’re heading to Masai Mara for an unforgettable safari, flying over the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Kenya, or relaxing at the beautiful Diani Beach, we provide seamless connections. Quick transfers to Nairobi for business or leisure are just as easy, and for a unique bird-watching experience, we can take you to Lake Nakuru. Wherever your journey takes you, enjoy the convenience and luxury of flying on your own schedule.

Safari Flight Operations

Experience Kenya’s breathtaking landscapes from the sky with our safari flight operations. Offering exclusive aerial access to the country's most iconic wildlife destinations, our flights provide a unique perspective of the natural beauty below. Whether it’s a scenic tour over Masai Mara, a trip to the Amboseli National Park for a view of Mount Kilimanjaro, or a journey to the Tsavo for its diverse wildlife, we ensure a comfortable, unforgettable experience. Let us take you to the heart of Kenya’s wildlife, swiftly and in style, with personalized flight options to suit your safari needs.
